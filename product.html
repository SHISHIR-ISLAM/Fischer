    German Engineered, Controllable Electric Heaters

    Fischer dynamic clay core heaters are the perfect heating solution for any home.     With Room By Room Heating Control

    Fischer heaters are manufactured to allow a constant warm air flow around your rooms, and their efficiency has been tested and verified by three independent bodies. Older, conventional heaters are large, bulky, unattractive and waste energy by pushing the warm air up to the ceiling.

    Fischer heaters are not night storage heaters. Instead, they are dynamic, direct acting storage heaters. They are built with surface fluting, which radiates heat from a much larger surface area, also drawing in cold air from the floor and distributing it evenly throughout your home.
